Ultimate Guide: Choosing the Right Gaming Laptop

Whether you're diving into competitive eSports or exploring AAA open-world games, the right gaming laptop can make all the difference. Below, we've broken down what specs matter for different genres and highlighted some user feedback on top models.

Recommended Specs by Game Type

eSports (Valorant, CS2, Fortnite)

  • CPU: Intel i5 / Ryzen 5 or better
  • GPU: GTX 1660 / RTX 3050 or higher
  • RAM: 8GB or more
  • Display: 144Hz for competitive edge

AAA Titles (Cyberpunk, Elden Ring)

  • CPU: Intel i7+ / Ryzen 7+
  • GPU: RTX 3060 / RX 6700M or higher
  • RAM: 16GB (32GB ideal)
  • Display: QHD/4K, 165Hz+ if possible

Creative + Gaming (Streaming, Editing)

  • CPU: Intel i9 / Ryzen 9
  • GPU: RTX 4070+
  • RAM: 32GB+
  • Storage: 1TB NVMe SSD
